3 points regarding Tawheed

Two meanings of Tawheed:

  1. Linguistic Meaning: To single out something and make it one
  2. Islamic Meaning: To single out Allah in every matter which is exclusive to him, including; Allah's attributes of Lordship, Allah's right to Worship, Allah's Names and Attributes

3 points Regarding Tawheed

  1. Allah has created us, provideds for us, and he has not left us without a purpose, rather, Allah has sent to us a messenger. Whoever obeys him, will enter paradise and whoever disobeys him will enter the fire.
  2. Allah is not pleased that anyone associated with him in his worship; not a close angel nor a sent messenger, proof to this is
    وَأَنَّ ٱلْمَسَـٰجِدَ لِلَّهِ فَلَا تَدْعُوا۟ مَعَ ٱللَّهِ" أَحَدًا Meaning: The places of worship are ˹only˺ for Allah, so do not invoke anyone besides Him.

Why has Allah sent us messengers?

  • To establish proof upon creation
  • As a mercy to mankind
